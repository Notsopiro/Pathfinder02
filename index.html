About Path Finder

Our Aim

To develop a user-friendly web platform that assists students and youth in discovering the most suitable career paths by analyzing their interests, aptitude, and personality traits using quizzes and AI-powered recommendations.

Objectives

  • Design a structured quiz system to evaluate user interests and cognitive strengths.
  • Build an AI chatbot that analyzes quiz responses and chats with users to understand their goals and preferences.
  • Foster a community for users to connect, share, and support each other's career journeys.
  • Offer detailed information about various careers including required qualifications, skillsets, and job outlooks.
  • Ensure the platform is accessible and engaging for students from all backgrounds.
